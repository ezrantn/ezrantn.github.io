Who Am I

Hey there! I’m Ezra, a software engineer crafting digital solutions in the vibrant tech landscape of Indonesia. While my days are filled with writing code and solving complex problems, there’s more to my story than just bits and bytes.

Think of this website as my digital garden - a space where I cultivate ideas, share experiences, and maybe even help fellow developers along the way. Why create this space? Well, in a world where we often hide behind avatars and usernames, I wanted to create something authentically me.

Motivation

There’s something beautiful about the solitude of coding - just me, my thoughts, and the occasional bug that keeps me humble. My journey into software engineering wasn’t a straight path. I wasn’t the stereotypical “computer whiz” growing up; I was actually more of a class clown. But life has a funny way of leading you exactly where you need to be.

My guiding principle comes from a Japanese proverb: “七転び八起き” (Nana korobi ya oki) - “Fall seven times, rise eight.” It’s more than just a saying; it’s how I approach both coding and life. Every bug is a lesson, every failed deployment a stepping stone to better architecture.

What To Expect

This space will be a blend of technical deep-dives and personal insights. Expect articles about:

  • Backend development adventures
  • Cloud computing explorations
  • System design thought processes
  • Personal growth in tech
  • And occasionally, thoughts about life beyond the screen

I believe the best technical content comes with a human touch. So while we’ll dive deep into code, we’ll never lose sight of the human element that makes technology meaningful.

Consider this your invitation to join me on this journey. Whether you’re here to learn about software engineering, share experiences, or just curious about the person behind the code - welcome. Let’s build something interesting together.

さようなら (Sayōnara),

Ezra
