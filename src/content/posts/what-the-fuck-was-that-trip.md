I still remember it was around August when our campus held this program called KKL (Kuliah Kerja Lapangan). Basically, it's a field study where we visit other universities. At first, I was kinda worried about the cost because, by the end of the trip, we'd be visiting three countries: Vietnam, Thailand, and Singapore. Well... Singapore was only for a day since we were just transiting there.

Singapore was... not exactly a great day. More like a fucking exhausting one.

We landed at Singapore Changi Airport at around 3 PM, if I remember correctly, and everything was pure chaos. There were about 20 of us, and holy shit, that airport is huge. We spent what felt like forever just trying to find a place to take a shower. I swear, it felt like we walked half the airport just to get there.

Vietnam, on the other hand, was amazing. The first thing I did? I got absolutely wasted.

I bought a bottle of Jägermeister and drank it neat, straight from the bottle. Like... literally. Besides getting drunk, we also visited a university and two companies to learn about their technology, business operations, and all that good stuff. I even managed to do some successful flirting with a local Vietnamese girl there, which was a nice little bonus.

When we got back to the hotel, things got even crazier. We bought a bottle of vodka and four bottles of soju. Holy fuck, I drank way too much—especially the vodka. I was completely fucking blacked out.

That night, I ended up puking in the bathroom... and I didn't even realize one of my friends was puking right next to me. Looking back, it was fucking chaotic.

Out of all the places we visited, though, I probably miss Thailand the most.

We went to Big C (if I remember correctly), where I bought more alcohol—just a beer this time, thankfully. I also decided to wander off completely on my own. I literally just booked a Grab to some random area because I wanted to get my right ear pierced and explore the city by myself. Huge thanks to Piercing Me BKK in Bangkok for the piercing. Definitely one of the coolest memories from the trip.

Right after that, I walked around and noticed weed was fucking everywhere. Neon green signs at every corner. I’m not gonna lie, I was tempted to try it just for the hell of it, but my logical brain kicked in. The last thing I wanted was to get randomly drug tested or detained at the airport on my way back. Screw that, I’m too young for jail. So instead of getting high, my sober ass decided to wander into a local go-go bar area.

I walked in completely oblivious, only to find myself standing in a room packed with massive, heavily tattooed guys. Some of them were wearing sharp suits, looking like they were literally plotting a minor coup or running a local syndicate. My brain immediately went into fight-or-flight mode. Hyperbolic thoughts of getting kidnapped by some underground cartel flashed through my mind. They just stared me down, and I got the fuck out of there faster than an unhandled exception crashes a server.

To top off the absolute peak Thailand experience, I decided to shoot my shot and flirt with this gorgeous local girl I met later on. I was turning on the charm, thinking I was on a roll, until "she" finally opened her mouth to reply.

Holy shit. The voice that came out was a straight-up deep, baritone bass.

My inner radar went off instantly. Turns out, my flawless detection system completely failed to register that I was hitting on a ladyboy. A beautiful trap, but a trap nonetheless. My brain experienced an instant system crash right then and there.

Looking back, that whole trip was an absolute fever dream. From puking in unison in Vietnam, accidentally trespassing into a mafia-looking headquarter, to getting hit with a deep-voiced plot twist in Bangkok. What a fucking time to be alive.
