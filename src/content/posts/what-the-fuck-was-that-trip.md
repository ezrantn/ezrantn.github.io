I still remember it was around August when our campus held this program called **KKL (Kuliah Kerja Lapangan)**. Basically, it's a field study where we visit other universities. At first, I was kinda worried about the cost because, by the end of the trip, we'd be visiting **three countries**: Vietnam, Thailand, and Singapore. Well... Singapore was only for a day since we were just transiting there.

Singapore was... not exactly a _great_ day. More like a **fucking exhausting** one.

We landed at Singapore Changi Airport at around 3 PM, if I remember correctly, and everything was pure chaos. There were about 20 of us, and holy shit, that airport is **huge**. We spent what felt like forever just trying to find a place to take a shower. I swear, it felt like we walked half the airport just to get there.

Vietnam, on the other hand, was amazing. The first thing I did? I got absolutely **wasted**.

I bought a bottle of Jägermeister and drank it neat, straight from the bottle. Like... literally. Besides getting drunk, we also visited a university and two companies to learn about their technology, business operations, and all that good stuff.

When we got back to the hotel, things got even crazier. We bought a bottle of vodka and **four bottles of soju**. Holy fuck, I drank way too much—especially the vodka. I was **completely fucking blacked out**.

That night, I ended up puking in the bathroom... and I didn't even realize one of my friends was puking right next to me. Looking back, it was fucking chaotic. 😂

Out of all the places we visited, though, I probably miss **Thailand** the most.

We went to Big C (if I remember correctly), where I bought more alcohol—just a beer this time, thankfully. I also got my **right ear pierced**. Huge thanks to **Piercing Me BKK** in Bangkok for that. Definitely one of the coolest memories from the trip.
