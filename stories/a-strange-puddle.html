A Strange Puddle

May 30, 2026

In the year 2026, a young man named Arthur was walking down Blackwater Lane during a heavy fog. The town had been acting very peculiar lately, and the ocean smelled like rotten eggs and old copper.

Suddenly, Arthur stopped. In the middle of the cobblestone street was a puddle of very dark, oily water. It did not reflect the sky, but instead showed a sky full of purple stars and moving tentacles.

"How odd," said Arthur, who was a very brave fellow.

He took a stick and poked the puddle. To his immense surprise, the stick was instantly sucked out of his hand! He bent down to look closer, and a tiny, pale hand made of salt and seaweed reached out of the water and handed him a wet piece of parchment.

The note read:

Celecy is waking up. Do not look under your bed tonight.

Arthur was so startled that he dropped the note. At that exact moment, the entire street began to lean at a forty-five-degree angle. The houses turned into black coral, and the puddle expanded until it swallowed the whole neighborhood.

Arthur managed to climb a telephone pole just as the ocean water rushed through the streets, carrying mutated fish with human teeth. He decided then and there that he would never poke strange puddles again, and he lived on top of that telephone pole for three days until the tide went back down.
